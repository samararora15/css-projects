A.P.J Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man of India

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, renowned as the "People's President" and the "Missile Man of India," was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. Serving as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007, Kalam was not only an accomplished scientist but also a visionary leader. His pivotal role in advancing India's space and defense programs, particularly in ballistic missile technology, garnered widespread admiration. Beyond his scientific contributions, Kalam was admired for his humility and passion for education. He believed in fostering the potential of young minds, leaving an enduring impact on the nation's progress. His inspirational speeches and writings continue to motivate people across generations.
